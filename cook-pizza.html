Ingredients

  1. Pizza Dough: Makes enough dough for two 10-12 inch pizzas
  2. 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  3. 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) of active dry yeast
  4. 3/4 cups (490 g) bread flour
  5. tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven)
  6. 2 teaspoons salt
  7. 1 teaspoon sugar
  8. Pizza Ingredients
  9. Extra virgin olive oil
  10. cornmeal (to help slide the pizza onto the pizza stone)
  11. Tomato sauce (smooth, or puréed)
  12. Firm mozzarella cheese, grated
  13. Fresh soft mozzarella cheese, separated into small clumps
  14. Parmesan cheese, grated
  15. Mushrooms, very thinly sliced if raw, otherwise first sautée
  16. Onions, thinly sliced raw or caramelized
  17. Ham, thinly sliced
